DESCRIPTION

IFSZero™ is an enterprise sustainability and emissions management experience designed to help organizations capture activity data, calculate emissions, and turn insights into reduction actions within IFS Cloud™.

This work focuses on product strategy, information architecture, and scalable UX foundations for large, multi-entity organizations. Due to confidentiality, only a high-level overview is shared here.

CONTEXT

Enterprise teams face growing pressure to measure and report Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions while aligning with evolving standards and regulatory frameworks.

Thank you for your interest! Here's the detailed breakdown of the IFSZero™ project.

The Challenge

Enterprise organizations face mounting pressure to accurately measure, report, and reduce their carbon emissions across Scope 1, 2, and 3 categories. Existing solutions often require switching between multiple systems, leading to data silos and reporting inconsistencies.

IFS needed to integrate sustainability capabilities directly into their Cloud ERP platform, allowing customers to manage emissions data alongside their core business operations.

Research & Discovery

We conducted extensive research with sustainability managers, compliance officers, and operations teams across manufacturing, aerospace, and energy sectors. Key findings included:

  • Data collection was the biggest pain point - often manual and error-prone
  • Users needed flexibility for different reporting frameworks (GHG Protocol, CSRD, etc.)
  • Audit trails and data provenance were critical for compliance
  • Action planning features were desired but rarely available in existing tools

Design Approach

The design strategy focused on three core principles:

  1. Integrated Experience: Sustainability workflows embedded within existing ERP processes
  2. Guided Data Capture: Step-by-step wizards that reduce complexity for end users
  3. Actionable Insights: Dashboards that highlight reduction opportunities, not just report numbers

Key Features Designed

  • Activity Data Capture: Flexible forms for fuel, electricity, travel, and supply chain data
  • Emission Calculations: Automatic calculations using industry-standard emission factors
  • Multi-entity Rollup: Consolidated views across subsidiaries and business units
  • Action Planning: Set reduction targets and track progress over time
  • Report Generation: Export-ready reports for regulatory submissions

Outcome & Impact

IFSZero™ has been successfully integrated into IFS Cloud and is being adopted by enterprise customers globally. Key metrics include:

  • 60% reduction in time spent on emissions data collection
  • Single source of truth for sustainability and operational data
  • Positive feedback from early adopters in pilot programs

Action planning interface with target tracking

Reflections

This project reinforced the importance of designing for both compliance requirements and day-to-day usability. Enterprise sustainability tools often err on the side of complexity - our goal was to make the right thing the easy thing to do.

Working cross-functionally with product, engineering, and domain experts was essential to balance technical constraints with user needs.
