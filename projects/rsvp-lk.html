DESCRIPTION

A comprehensive event management and RSVP platform designed to streamline event planning and guest management in Sri Lanka.

CONTEXT

Created to address the growing need for digital event management solutions. The platform enables hosts to create, manage, and track event invitations while guests can easily RSVP and manage their event attendance.

The design focuses on simplicity and efficiency, making event coordination seamless for both organizers and attendees.
