DESCRIPTION

An interactive wishing portal designed for T20 World Cup fans to send wishes and messages of support to their favorite teams and players.

CONTEXT

Created during the T20 World Cup season to engage cricket fans worldwide. The portal provided an interactive platform for users to express their support and excitement for the tournament.

The design focused on creating an energetic and festive atmosphere that captured the spirit of cricket and the excitement of the World Cup tournament.
